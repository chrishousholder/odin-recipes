Pad Kra Pao

A delicious dish full of big flavors and big protein.

This is a dish that takes no time to make but tastes like it's been soaking in these flavors all day. The key is the punch from the chiles and the umami of the Thai basil.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of shredded tofu
  • 2 shallots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 small red chiles
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegan oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegan fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 bunch Thai basil

Steps

  1. shred the tofu and bake in the air fryer for 12 minutes
  2. combine soy sauces, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar and set aside.
  3. in a pan, fry the chopped shallot, garlic, and chili until fragrant.
  4. once softened, add the tofu and mix to combine
  5. add your sauce mixture and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. add the Thai basil just before removing from heat.
  7. serve over rice and sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onion.
