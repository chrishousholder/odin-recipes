Black Pepper Tofu

Just like your favorite Chinese takeout... But at home!

This dish is packed with flavor. The subtle punch of the black pepper pairs perfectly with the crunch of the tofu.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of extra firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 5 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cracked black pepper
  • 1 nub grated ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves, diced
  • 1 shallot, diced
  • 2 red chiles

Steps

  1. press excess moisture out of tofu and slice into 1 inch cubes. Toss with cornstarch until tofu is well coated.
  2. combine soy sauce, sugar, black pepper, and ginger in a small bowl and set aside.
  3. heat 2 tbsp of oil in a large skillet. Add tofu and cook for 7-8 minutes, making sure to brown all sides, then transfer tofu to a plate.
  4. add 1 tbsp oil to the pan with garlic, shallots, and chiles and cook until fragrant.
  5. add tofu back to the pan along with the black pepper sauce and cook for 2 minutes, tossing often.
  6. serve over rice and garnish with green onions and chili crisp.
