Black Pepper Tofu

Just like your favorite Chinese takeout... But at home!

This dish is packed with flavor. The subtle punch of the black pepper pairs perfectly with the crunch of the tofu.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of extra firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 5 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 nub grated ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves, diced
  • 1 shallot, diced
  • 2 red chiles
