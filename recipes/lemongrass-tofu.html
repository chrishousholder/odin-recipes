Lemongrass Tofu

Crispy pieces of tofu swimming in aromatics.

This dish is the perfect introduction to lemongrass's earthy flavor profile. It takes almost no time at all to prepare which will quickly make it one of your go to menu items each week.

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 4 short stalks lemongrass
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 red chiles
  • 1/2 red capsicum, diced
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tsp light soy sauce
  • oil for cooking

Steps

  1. press moisture out of tofu and tear into 1 inch pieces.
  2. heat oil in a large non-stick pan and fry tofu until golden brown on all sides. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. peel and remove the tough outer layer from the lemongrass. Slice and mince the remaining lemongrass
  4. add a generous amount of oil to the heated pan and pan fry the shallot, chiles, and capsicum until the shallot becomes translucent.
  5. add the lemongrass and fry for another 2 minutes until very fragrant
  6. add in the sugar and soy sauces. Mix well and lower the heat to medium.
  7. return tofu to the pan and mix well with the aromatics.
  8. deglaze the pan with 2 tbsp water.
  9. serve over rice with chopped green onions.
